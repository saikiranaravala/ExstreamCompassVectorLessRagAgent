Tracking revisions

Revision tracking lets you keep a record of the edits that you and other users make to text content in Authoring view. When you turn on revision tracking, you can view your changes as you make them. For example, deleted text appears with a line through it, and added text appears with an underline bracket. All text changes appear in the Revisions panel.

To turn on revision tracking:

  1. Click . This option is available only in Authoring view.

  2. Make a revision to the content.

  3. On the toolbar, use the revision tracking tools to perform the tasks described in the following table:

    Task Action
    Choose how revisions are shown

    Choose one of the three options in the Revision view list:

    • Original—Shows the original text without edits.
    • Markup—Shows text edits with changes marked.

      Note: When revision tracking is on, Content Author automatically switches to this mode when you make edits to content.

    • No Markup—Shows text edits as if they were accepted.
    Navigate between revisions Click , , , and . These options let you navigate to the first, previous, next, and last revisions in your content.
    Accept or reject revisions Click or . Note that you can also accept or reject revisions in the Revisions panel.
    Show or hide the Revisions panel

    Click . This option shows each revision individually in a panel on the left side of the screen. You can navigate to a revision by clicking its value in the Revisions panel.

    Note that Content Author automatically assigns one of five colors to each reviewer's revisions in order to help you quickly differentiate between reviewers.

Important: You must accept all tracked revisions before you can submit content for approval.
